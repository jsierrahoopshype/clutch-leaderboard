Methodology

A clutch event has PERIOD ≥ 4 and the absolute score margin ≤ 5. The TIME WINDOW selector further restricts which moments count: Last 5:00 (the classic NBA clutch window), Last 1:00 (final minute of regulation or any OT), or Last 0:10 (final-shot territory). Tighter windows are subsets of wider ones. PPG = clutch points / clutch games (games in which the player had at least one event in the current window).
