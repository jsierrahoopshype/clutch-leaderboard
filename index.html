METHODOLOGY

A clutch event is any play with ≥ 4th quarter and ≤ 5:00 remaining in that period, while the absolute score margin is ≤ 5. Score margin carries forward between scoring plays. All overtime minutes qualify (margin filter still applies). PPG = clutch points / clutch games (games in which the player had at least one clutch shot, FT, or turnover).
